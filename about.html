Avoid the Monkey Dentist

Feed your monkey healthly fruits and vegetables.

Why feed your monkey vegetables?

A healthy monkey diet is the key mitigator to unhealthy teeth and the need to see the crazy monkey dentist.


Here is a list of appropriate foods to feed your monkey.


Carrots
Brocolli
Potatoes (Cooked)
Cabbage
Corn on the Cob
Lettuce
Bananas (Infrequently)
Cauliflower
Kale
Beets (Cooked)
Sweet Potatoes (Cooked)
Seaweed

Here is a list of inappropriate foods not to feed your monkey.


Candy
Ice Cream
Soda
Cookies
Automotive Fluids
Ducklings
